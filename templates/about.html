Origin story

I tend to lose stuff a lot. When I moved to Dhaka about two months ago. Just in the first month, I lost a phone, a calculator a headphone, and two sets of keys. So that's when the idea for my final project came. A website where people can post their lost items and if someone finds them who can look them up. Kinda like bikroy.com but for lost items. I designed a lot of the elements with green and red colors to create a BD aesthetic.
